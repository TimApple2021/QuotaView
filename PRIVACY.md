# Privacy Policy

QuotaView is a local macOS utility. It reads supported local files, local client state, and locally available official status responses from the user's own Mac. It stores its derived dashboard, settings, scan cache, and history locally in the user's Application Support directory.

QuotaView does not upload logs, prompts, replies, token history, cost history, quota snapshots, credentials, cookies, or account identifiers. It does not send telemetry or analytics. It does not read `auth.json`, Keychain secrets, OAuth tokens, passwords, or cookies. It does not call reset, redeem, consume, or write-back operations against official services.

The app may start a local bundled scanner to read client data and update the local dashboard. API-equivalent cost is a local estimate and is not official billing information. Local history may be incomplete compared with an account-wide provider total.

Users control all local files and may remove the app and its Application Support data independently. For security reports, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
